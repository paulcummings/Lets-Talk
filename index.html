Welcome to Let's Talk!

What Are We?

Let's Talk! is a video and texting platform for people who want users to improve their speaking abilities in their non-native language by chatting with native speakers.

COMMUNICATION

Communication is an essential part of our everyday life. We want to help people bridge the language barrier by connecting them with native speakers across the web.

EFFICIENT

Don't have time to learn a new language? Let's Talk! lets you hop into a conversation for a couple minutes. Explore, learn and chat away. It's that easy!
